# Four failure modes emerge without development structure

<div class="card-grid">
  <div class="card">
    <div class="card-title">Context evaporates</div>
    <div class="card-body">Sessions lose decisions and state. You re-explain constantly.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <div class="card-title">No roles, no boundaries</div>
    <div class="card-body">One Claude touches everything — no ownership or separation.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <div class="card-title">No gates or guardrails</div>
    <div class="card-body">Code ignores your conventions and security rules. No checkpoint.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <div class="card-title">No learning loop</div>
    <div class="card-body">Mistakes repeat every run. Patterns vanish when sessions end.</div>
  </div>
</div>
